The Experience

One day in the mountains. Clarity for the year ahead.

Everything is taken care of, you just show up. I pick you up in Dublin and we head into the mountains of Ireland, me and my dog Finn. As we hike, we go deep into every part of your life, health, love, relationships and finances, and turn the fog into clear, written goals. Then I keep you on track for six months.

  • Picked up in Dublin, a full day in the Irish mountains (09:00–17:00)
  • One-on-one on the trail with me and Finn
  • We go deep on health, love, relationships and finances
  • You leave with clear, written goals, not vague intentions
  • 6 one-hour check-ins over the next 6 months to keep you moving
