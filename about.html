About

Hi, I'm Floris.

I moved from the Netherlands to Dublin with my dog Finn to start a new chapter at Google, where I work in tech and AI. But my best moments happen off the screen. You'll find me on a mountain trail, leading hikes, exploring new places, and helping people reconnect with nature, and with themselves.

My story

The best opportunities happen outside.

I've spent years building communities, supporting entrepreneurs, leading innovation programs and helping people create meaningful change.

But the biggest lesson I've learned is simple: some of life's most important conversations happen while walking through forests, climbing mountains or sitting around a campfire. The best opportunities in life often arrive the moment you step outside.

GoDoIt is my attempt to create more of those moments, whether that's through a hike, a conversation, a retreat or a shared adventure. It brings together everything I care about: adventure, nature, personal growth, meaningful connection and entrepreneurship.

Let's go.
